10-Day Uttarakhand Itinerary

From Mumbai to Jim Corbett, Nainital, Mussoorie & Rishikesh

Day 1: 11 Jan

Mumbai to Delhi by Garib Rath Express

Evening departure from Mumbai by Garib Rath Express (approx 8:15 PM). Overnight train journey to Delhi.

Day 2: 12 Jan

Delhi to Jim Corbett National Park

Morning arrival in Delhi. Transfer to Jim Corbett (6-7 hours). Check into resort. Overnight stay. Enjoy Pool Party With DJ

Day 3: 13 Jan

Jim Corbett to Nainital

Morning safari, Corbett Musuem. Afternoon drive to Nainital (3 hours). Check into hotel. Overnight stay at.

Day 4: 14 Jan

Nainital to Mussoorie (Overnight Journey)

Enjoy Boating at nainital, Naini Lake, Mall road snow view point and explore other local places and Evening departure from Nainital after dinner. Overnight journey to Mussoorie by private bus (approx 6 hours). Arrive morning next day ro mussorie.

Day 5: 15 Jan

Mussoorie Sightseeing

Full day exploring Kempty Falls, Gun Hill, Camel's Back Road. Check into hotel. Optional cable car ride. First overnight stay in Mussoorie.

Day 6: 16 Jan

Mussoorie Leisure Day

Day at leisure for optional activities - Company Garden, Mall Road shopping, or relaxing at the hotel. Second overnight stay in Mussoorie.

Day 7: 17 Jan

Mussoorie to Rishikesh

Morning drive to Rishikesh (4 hours).Morning river rafting.Evening Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat. Riverside Camping , Overnight stay and Poolparty with bonfire at camp.

Day 8: 18 Jan

Rishikesh to Delhi via Haridwar

Visit Laxman Jhula, Ramjhula. Afternoon excursion to Haridwar for Har Ki Pauri Ganga Aarti. Overnight drive to Delhi (5-6 hours).

Day 9: 19 Jan

Delhi to Mumbai by Garib Rath

Reach Delhi. Board Garib Rath Express to Mumbai. Arrive next morning with unforgettable memories.
